Flag

The Pakistani flag is dark green with a white crescent, a five-pointed star, and a white vertical stripe. The flag was designed by Syed Amir-uddin Kedwaii and adopted in 1947.

Green: Represents Islam, hope, joy, and love
White: Represents peace, honesty, and the role of religious minorities
Crescent: Represents progress
Star: Represents enlightenment

The flag was based on the All-India Muslim League flag It was adopted on August 11, 1947, three days before Pakistan gained independence It is flown on important days like Independence Day and Republic Day The flag is referred to as Parcham-e-Sitāra-o-Hilāl in the national anthem, which means "Flag of the Crescent and Star"
