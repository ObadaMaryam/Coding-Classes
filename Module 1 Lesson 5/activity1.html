Facts about Pets

Cats have over one hundred vocal sounds, while dogs only have about ten. A pack of kitten is called a "kindle" , while a pack of adult cats is called a "clowder".

Birds are vertebrates that have adapted for flight.The ostrich is the largest bird, while the bee hummingbird is the smallest. Birds communicate with a variety of call notes.
